## 🔍 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the app work without internet?**
The app caches the last known rates. However, you need an internet connection to refresh the data and get the most recent prices.

**Is my data private?**
The app does not collect personal data. It only sends requests to fetch public exchange rate data.

**How do I remove the app?**
Close the application first. Open your Applications folder, find the app, and move it to the Trash. You can also remove the local cache files located in your Library folder if you want a clean removal.

**What should I do if the app crashes?**
Shut down the app completely. Wait a few seconds and launch it again. If the issue persists, check that your internet connection is stable.
